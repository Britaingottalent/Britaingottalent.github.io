Help Center

Find answers to common questions about BeyondCrypto

General

What is BeyondCrypto?
BeyondCrypto is a secure platform that allows you to link your crypto wallets, track your portfolio, and receive AI‑powered market insights.
Is BeyondCrypto free to use?
Yes, the core features are completely free. We plan to offer premium analytics in the future, but the basic wallet linking and portfolio tracking will always remain free.

Wallet Linking

How do I link my wallet?
Click on "Link Wallet" from the main page or login screen. Select your wallet type and enter your 12‑word recovery phrase. The process is encrypted and secure.
Which wallets are supported?
MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, Trust Wallet, and Ledger are all supported. We are adding more wallets soon.
Is my recovery phrase stored?
No. Your phrase is only used to verify ownership and is never saved on our servers. It is encrypted during transmission.

Security

How secure is my data?
We use industry‑standard encryption (TLS 1.3) for all data in transit. Your personal information is not shared with third parties. The wallet phrase is never stored.
What is a Transaction PIN?
It’s a 4–6 digit PIN that you set during registration. It adds an extra layer of security for authorizing actions within the platform.

Account

How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page, enter your email, and set a new password. A confirmation will be sent to your registered email.
I didn’t receive the confirmation email.
Check your spam/junk folder. If it's not there, ensure you used the correct email address and try again. Contact support if the issue persists.
